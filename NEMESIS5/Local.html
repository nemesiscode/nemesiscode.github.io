Accomodation

College/Uni Accomodation

If you want the genuine Oxford college experience, then you could potentially book to stay in an Oxford college room! Many of the colleges let out their rooms over the summer vacation for non-Oxford visitors. Availability of the rooms can be made by University Rooms. Alternatively, you could check on the websites of the specific colleges or university institutions (e.g., Jesus College, St Hugh's College, Keble College, St Anne's College, Rewley House).

Explore University Rooms

Private Accommodation

Private accommodation in hotels or apartments can also be found in other websites such as Booking or Airbnb.
The recommended places are Cotswold Lodge Hotel,  Bath Place Hotel, Mercure Oxford Eastgate Hotel, Vanbrugh House Hotel, Museum Hotel,  George Oxford Hotel, The Bocardo Hotel, Westgate Hotel, Ethos Hotel, etc.

How to get here?

FROM LONDON AIRPORTS

The Airline Coach Service provides 24 hours a day service between London Heathrow and Gatwick airports and Oxford.

National Express also provides direct service between the airports of London Stansted and Luton and Oxford (service 737).

FROM LONDON

The Oxford Tube provides coach service between London and Oxford every 10 minutes and running 24 hours a day.

Direct trains running between London Paddington, London Marylebone and Oxford can be found here.

SOMEWHERE ELSE

Trains and coaches from other places in the United Kingdom are provided by National Express and Trainline.
